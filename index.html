About

My name is Andrea and I am originally from Taipei, Taiwan but have relocated to Santa Clara, CA. Currently a student at Santa Clara University. I hope to continue my education in Business, more specifically Management. As an individual, I am hardworking to get the job done with detail and focus, which is developed through the numerous responsibilities in event planning and other activities. From the different activities, I have developed the skill to be a team player with desire to hear the opinions of others, especially when I was responsible for leading others in various organizations. As someone who has lived abroad and has come in contact with many diverse individuals, I have learned languages such as: English, Spanish, Chinese, and Taiwanese fluently.
